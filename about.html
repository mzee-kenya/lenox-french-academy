About Kirinyaga French Academy

Mission

This demo showcases a small, practical sequence of French lessons from A1 to B2. The focus is on clear, interactive examples: pronunciation, short quizzes, and a lightweight unlock/progression model to illustrate learner flow.

Pedagogy

  • Micro-lessons with practicing and listening opportunities.
  • Short formative quizzes that reveal the next topic when passed.
  • Use of spaced practice and repetition (demonstrated via speech playback).

Contributors

Prepared for demonstration purposes. Content is a sample curriculum and can be extended with more exercises, audio recordings, and multimedia.
