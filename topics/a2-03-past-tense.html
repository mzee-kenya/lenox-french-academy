A2-03: Passé Composé (Past Tense)

📘 Quick Explanation

The passé composé is used to talk about completed actions in the past. It often uses an auxiliary verb (avoir or être) + past participle.

J'ai mangé une pomme. — I ate an apple.

Elle est allée au marché. — She went to the market.
