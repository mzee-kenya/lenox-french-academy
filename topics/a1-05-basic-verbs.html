A1-05: Basic Verbs

This lesson introduces essential French verbs used in daily life, their conjugations, and how to apply them both directly and indirectly.

Être – To be

Je suisI am
Tu esYou are
Il/Elle estHe/She is
Nous sommesWe are
Vous êtesYou are (formal/plural)
Ils/Elles sontThey are
Il est étudiant. (He is a student)

Avoir – To have

J’aiI have
Tu asYou have
Il/Elle aHe/She has
Nous avonsWe have
Vous avezYou have
Ils/Elles ontThey have
Elle a une voiture. (She has a car)
Il a besoin d’aide. (He needs help)

Aller – To go

Je vaisI go
Tu vasYou go
Il/Elle vaHe/She goes
Nous allonsWe go
Vous allezYou go
Ils/Elles vontThey go
Je vais à la maison. (I’m going home)

Faire – To do / To make

Je faisI do
Tu faisYou do
Il/Elle faitHe/She does
Nous faisonsWe do
Vous faitesYou do
Ils/Elles fontThey do
Elle fait du sport. (She does sports)
Je fais attention à toi. (I pay attention to you)

Parler – To speak

Je parleI speak
Tu parlesYou speak
Il/Elle parleHe/She speaks
Nous parlonsWe speak
Vous parlezYou speak
Ils/Elles parlentThey speak
Tu parles français ? (Do you speak French?)
